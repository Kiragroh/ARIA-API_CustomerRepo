Resource Profile: Condition-Diagnosis (CancerCondition)

Official URL: http://varian.com/fhir/v1/StructureDefinition/CancerCondition Version: 1.0.0
Active as of 2024-07-26 Computable Name: Condition_Diagnosis

Copyright/Legal: Varian Medical Systems, Inc.

This resource is used to record detailed information about a diagnosis that has risen to a level of concern. The condition could be a point in time diagnosis in context of an encounter, it could be an item on the practitioner’s Problem List, or it could be a concern that doesn’t exist on the practitioner’s Problem List.

Usage:

  • Examples for this Resource Profile: Condition/Condition-DX-94100000000000000149-12 and Condition/RadiotherapyConditionSHS-1
