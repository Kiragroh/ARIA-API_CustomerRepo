Resource Profile: Practitioner

Official URL: http://varian.com/fhir/v1/StructureDefinition/Practitioner Version: 1.0.0
Active as of 2021-02-02 Computable Name: Practitioner

Copyright/Legal: Varian Medical Systems, Inc.

Practitioner covers all individuals who are engaged in the healthcare process and healthcare-related services as part of their formal responsibilities and this Resource is used for attribution of activities and responsibilities to these individuals. Practitioners include (but are not limited to):

physicians, dentists, pharmacists, physician assistants, nurses, scribes, midwives, dietitians, therapists, optometrists, paramedics, medical technicians, laboratory scientists, prosthetic technicians, radiographers, social workers, professional homecare providers, official volunteers, receptionists handling patient registration

Practitioner represents Doctor and Staff configured in ARIA.

Doctor/Staff demographics, role and association with department is being represented.

Need to track doctors, staff, locums etc. for both healthcare practitioners, funders, etc.

Usage:

  • Refer to this Resource Profile: AllergyIntolerance, Appointment, Audit Event, Condition-Diagnosis (CancerCondition)... Show 13 more, CarePlan, CareTeam, ChargeItem, A reference to a document, Group, Observation-StageCategory, Observation-StageGroup, Task, Observation-Vital-Signs, Observation-Vital-Signs-Component, documentreference-signature, documentreference-supervisor and lastModificationUser
  • Examples for this Resource Profile: Practitioner/Practitioner-1258 and Practitioner/RadiotherapyPractitionerSHS-1005
